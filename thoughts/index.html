As a result of both being an introvert and having professors who seem to enjoy requiring me to sit in classes, then neglecting to provide me with any new information, I think a lot. Specifically, I talk to myself, and write poorly-edited essays in my head. Since I also happen to be an archivist, I have created this space to allow me to forever preserve my ramblings.

I make a point of avoiding anything more than minimal editing in my writing, so that it sounds similar to how I speak.

I avoid calling this a blog, as that would lead people to believe:

  • this will be updated frequently
  • events of my life will appear here
  • I have something to say that you will find interesting

I guarantee none of these (and am inclined to say that the 2nd item will never happen).

For the same reason, I don't anticipate ever creating an RSS feed. If I do, then I'd start to only publish things that I think people will want to read, and I wish to avoid that.
